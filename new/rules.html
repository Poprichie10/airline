RULES OF THE COMPANY

At Pop Richie Airline we have certain rules and regulations in place to ensure a fair and secure flight booking experience for all users. These guidelines are designed to maintain a high standard of service and protect the rights and interests of both customers and our company. Here are some key rules and regulations that apply to our flight website:

1. Accurate Information: We strive to provide accurate and up-to-date flight information on our platform. However, we cannot guarantee the accuracy of all data as it is sourced from various airlines and travel providers. It is the responsibility of users to verify the details of their flights before making a booking.

2. Booking Policies: Users must comply with the booking policies and terms and conditions set by the airlines and travel providers. This includes adhering to baggage regulations, check-in procedures, and any other requirements specified by the airline.

3. Payment and Pricing: Users are responsible for providing accurate payment information and ensuring that they have sufficient funds to complete the booking. All prices displayed on our website are subject to availability and may change without notice. Users are advised to double-check the final price, including any additional fees or charges, before finalizing their booking.

4. Fair Usage: Users are expected to utilize our platform for legitimate and personal use only. Any misuse, fraudulent activities, or attempt to manipulate the system may result in the termination of the user's account and legal action, if necessary.

5. Privacy and Data Protection: We take user privacy and data protection seriously and maintain strict measures to safeguard personal information. Users are encouraged to review our Privacy Policy to understand how their data is collected, stored, and shared.

6. Intellectual Property: The content and materials displayed on our website are protected by copyright and other intellectual property laws. Users are not allowed to copy, reproduce, or distribute any content from our website without prior permission.

7. Modification and Cancellation: Users should familiarize themselves with our modification and cancellation policies, as well as those of the airlines and travel providers. Changes and cancellations may be subject to fees, restrictions, and availability.

These rules and regulations are in place to ensure a fair and transparent flight booking experience for all users. By using our website, users agree to abide by these guidelines and accept any consequences for non-compliance. We reserve the right to update or modify these rules and regulations as needed, and users are encouraged to review them periodically.
